## What is NDPR?

The Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) was issued by the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA) in 2019. It governs how organisations collect, process, store, and share personal data of Nigerian citizens. Non-compliance attracts fines of up to 2% of annual gross revenue or 10 million naira whichever is higher.

## Key NDPR Requirements

1. Obtain lawful consent before collecting personal data
2. Publish a clear and accessible privacy policy
3. Appoint a Data Protection Officer if processing large volumes of data
4. Report data breaches to NITDA within 72 hours
5. Conduct a Data Protection Impact Assessment for high risk processing
6. Engage only NDPR compliant data processors
