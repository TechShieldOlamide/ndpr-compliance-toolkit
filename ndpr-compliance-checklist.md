## 1. Lawful Basis for Processing

- [ ] All personal data processing activities have a lawful basis
- [ ] Consent is obtained before collecting personal data
- [ ] Consent is freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous
- [ ] Consent records are maintained
- [ ] Users can withdraw consent at any time
- [ ] Processing without consent is documented with legal justification

---

## 2. Privacy Policy

- [ ] Privacy policy is published and accessible
- [ ] Privacy policy written in clear and plain language
- [ ] Privacy policy states what data is collected
- [ ] Privacy policy states why data is collected
- [ ] Privacy policy states how data is used
- [ ] Privacy policy states how long data is retained
- [ ] Privacy policy states who data is shared with
- [ ] Privacy policy includes contact details for data complaints
- [ ] Privacy policy is reviewed and updated annually

---

## 3. Data Subject Rights

- [ ] Process in place for handling data subject access requests
- [ ] Process in place for handling data deletion requests
- [ ] Process in place for handling data correction requests
- [ ] Process in place for handling data portability requests
- [ ] Response to data subject requests completed within 30 days
- [ ] Data subject rights process communicated to users

---

## 4. Data Protection Officer

- [ ] DPO appointed if processing personal data on a large scale
- [ ] DPO contact details published in privacy policy
- [ ] DPO has appropriate knowledge of data protection law
- [ ] DPO reports directly to senior management
- [ ] DPO role and responsibilities documented

---

## 5. Data Processing Activities

- [ ] Record of processing activities maintained
- [ ] All third party processors identified
- [ ] Data processing agreements in place with all processors
- [ ] Cross border data transfers documented and justified
- [ ] Data minimisation principle applied

---

## 6. Data Security

- [ ] Personal data encrypted at rest
- [ ] Personal data encrypted in transit
- [ ] Access to personal data restricted to authorised staff
- [ ] Security measures reviewed annually
- [ ] Staff trained on data protection obligations

---

## 7. Data Breach Management

- [ ] Data breach response procedure documented
- [ ] Breach notification process to NDPC within 72 hours
- [ ] Breach notification process to affected individuals documented
- [ ] Breach register maintained
- [ ] All breaches and near misses logged

---

## 8. Data Retention

- [ ] Data retention policy documented
- [ ] Retention periods defined for each data category
- [ ] Process in place for secure data deletion
- [ ] Retention periods communicated in privacy policy

---

## 9. Third Party Processors

- [ ] All third party processors identified and documented
- [ ] Due diligence conducted on all processors
- [ ] Data processing agreements signed with all processors
- [ ] Processor compliance reviewed annually

---

## 10. NDPC Registration

- [ ] Organisation registered with NDPC if required
- [ ] Annual data protection audit filed with NDPC
- [ ] Licensed Data Protection Compliance Organisation engaged if required
