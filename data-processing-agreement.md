## 2. Definitions

**Personal Data** means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

**Processing** means any operation performed on personal data including collection, storage, use, disclosure, or deletion.

**Data Subject** means the individual whose personal data is being processed.

**Breach** means any accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorised disclosure of, or access to personal data.

## 5. Obligations of the Processor

The Processor agrees to:

**5.1** Process personal data only on documented instructions from the Controller.

**5.2** Ensure that all staff authorised to process personal data are bound by confidentiality obligations.

**5.3** Implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect personal data.

**5.4** Not engage any sub-processor without prior written consent from the Controller.

**5.5** Assist the Controller in responding to data subject rights requests.

**5.6** Notify the Controller without undue delay and within 24 hours of becoming aware of a personal data breach.

**5.7** Delete or return all personal data to the Controller upon termination of this agreement.

**5.8** Make available all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with this agreement.

**5.9** Not transfer personal data outside Nigeria without prior written authorisation from the Controller.

---

## 6. Obligations of the Controller

The Controller agrees to:

**6.1** Provide documented instructions for all processing activities.

**6.2** Ensure that personal data shared with the Processor is accurate and lawfully collected.

**6.3** Notify the Processor of any changes to processing instructions promptly.

**6.4** Ensure that data subjects have been informed of the processing in accordance with NDPR requirements.

## 9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach the Processor must:

1. Notify the Controller within 24 hours of becoming aware
2. Provide full details of the breach including nature, data affected, and measures taken
3. Cooperate fully with the Controller in managing the breach and notifying NITDA

---

## 10. Termination

Upon termination of this agreement the Processor must within 30 days:

- Delete all personal data processed under this agreement
- Provide written confirmation of deletion to the Controller
- Return any physical materials containing personal data

---

## 11. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria including the NDPR 2019 and the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023.
